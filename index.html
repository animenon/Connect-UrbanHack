Connect

Connect is a web site built for easier lodging of FIRs, registering complaints of other govt. organizations and to encourage use of public transport.

  We have a direct First Information Report Lodging System which can be used to lodge complaints regarding any criminal offences seen or known, this makes it easier to lodge complaints as you don't have to personally visit the Police Station.
